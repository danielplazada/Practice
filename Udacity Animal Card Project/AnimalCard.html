Rabbit

Rabbits are small, furry mammals with long ears,
short fluffy tails, and strong, large hind legs.
They have 2 pairs of sharp incisors (front teeth),
one pair on top and one pair on the bottom. ...
Their teeth are specifically adapted for gnawing
and grow continuously throughout their lives.


What do i eat?


  • Item #1: Hay
  • Item #2: Carrots
  • Item #3: Pellets
  • Item #4: Water

Happy rabbits practice a cute behavior known as a
“binky:” they jump up in the air and twist and spin
around! A baby rabbit is called a kit, a female is
called a doe, and a male is a buck. A group of rabbits
is called a herd. Rabbits are herbivores, eating a diet
entirely of grasses and other plants.
